What We Do?

We like to think of our handyman services as more of a general contractor. We do everything except septic an electrical work. We make sure every customer is satisfied and every jobsite looks like we were never there before we leave. We always leave the customer smiling.

Deck Building & Repairs

We do new deck construction and decking repair, from the ground up or just a stain job.

Fence Building & Repairs

We do new fence construction as well as fence repairs. From the ground up, or just a paint job.

Patio Construction

We do new patio construction as well as patio repairs and resurfacing. Concrete or brick!

New Drywall & Repairs

We do new drywall hanging, as well as hole and entire wall repairs. We also install door stopers!

Water Damage Repair

We replace drywall, carpet and do wall resotations. Includes blowers, dryers and moisture absorbers.

Roofing Services

We do new roof construction as well as roofing repair. We use 30 year roofing compisition

Concrete & Sidewalks

We do new sidewalk construction as well as sidewalk repairs. Also do great brick walking paths.

Plumbing Services

We install sinks, bathtubs, showers, toilets and garbage disposals. No sewer or septic.
